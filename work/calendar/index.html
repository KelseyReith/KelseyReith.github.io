Calendar

Fall 2019

This a calendar that breaks the conventions of the wall hanging, grid structured calendar. It's inspired by the game of Dungeons and Dragons, hence why the calendar takes the form of a twelve sided die which each face representing a month of the year while depicting a class one can play in the game. Rather than setting the numbered days of the month on a grid, the numbers reflect the imagery of each class.
